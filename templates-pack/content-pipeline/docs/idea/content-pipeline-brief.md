# Content-pipeline brief

Replace this file with your own brief. The outliner reads it end-to-end and
emits `content-pipeline-chapters.json`.

## What is this

One paragraph: what you're writing, who it's for, and why now.

## Audience

- Who picks this up?
- What do they already know?
- What's the change in them you're hoping to cause?

## Voice / house style

- Register: formal / conversational / technical / casual.
- Tense and person.
- Things you definitely don't want to sound like.

## Through-line

The single thread that runs from chapter 1 to the end. If a chapter doesn't
serve the through-line, it doesn't exist.

## Non-goals

What this is *not*. Glossaries, appendices, or related-work chapters that the
outliner should not invent.
