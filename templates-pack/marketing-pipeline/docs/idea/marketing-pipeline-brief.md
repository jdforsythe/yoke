# Marketing-pipeline brief

The brand guide / house voice for this campaign. The copywriter inherits this
file as `{{architecture_md}}`; the brand-voice reviewer enforces it. Replace
this with your real brand voice before running.

## Campaign

- What you're launching, why now, and the one big asset (signup, demo, docs).

## Voice

- Register: peer-to-peer / professional / playful / authoritative.
- Tense and person.
- Things you definitely don't sound like (be explicit — "no growth-hacker
  bro speak," "no purple prose," "no exclamation points," etc.).

## Claim discipline

- What you can claim without proof (general value statements).
- What you cannot claim without a citation (numbers, comparative claims,
  testimonials).
- What you cannot claim at all (regulated language, unverified outcomes).

## Channel rules

- Subject lines: ≤ 60 characters, no all-caps, no excessive emoji.
- Landing-page hero: one sentence + one CTA, no multi-CTA pages.
- Paid social: lead with the persona's pain, not the brand's name.

## CTA mapping

- Where each persona's variants should drive (free signup, docs, pricing).
